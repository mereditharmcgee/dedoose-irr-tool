Dedoose Inter-Rater Reliability

Drop two or three coded Dedoose .docx exports of the same transcript and get per-code Cohen's or Fleiss kappa, a publication-ready xlsx report, and a calibration document for the passages where your coders disagreed.

Everything runs in your browser. Your files are never uploaded, never sent to a server, and never stored. Refresh the page and it is gone.

De-identify your transcripts first. This tool reads the files you give it; it does not remove names or other identifying details. Strip participant identifiers from your transcripts before you open them here, in line with your IRB protocol. Processing locally keeps the file on your own computer, but it does not anonymize the content for you.
